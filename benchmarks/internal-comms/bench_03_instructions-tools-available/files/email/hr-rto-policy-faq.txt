Following feedback from the last engagement survey, we're finalizing our updated
hybrid work policy. Starting September 1, 2026, employees will move to a hybrid
schedule: in-office Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, with Monday and Friday remote.

Q: Do we need to be in the office 5 days a week?
A: No. The policy is hybrid - 3 days in office (Tue-Thu), 2 days remote.

Q: Does this apply to every team?
A: Yes, this applies company-wide unless your manager has an approved fully-remote
exception on file with People team.

Q: When exactly does this start?
A: September 1, 2026. We'll share office capacity and desk booking details closer
to the date.

Reach out to your People partner if you have questions specific to your situation.
