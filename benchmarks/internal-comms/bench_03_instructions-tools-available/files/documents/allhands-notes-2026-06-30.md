## Q&A Highlights

A few variations of the same question came in through the Q&A form and in
Slack threads in the days after the Atlas launch delay was announced, so
we're capturing the leadership answer here for visibility:

Q: With the Atlas launch pushed back two months, should we be worried about
a hiring freeze or layoffs?
A (Alicia Moreno, CEO): "We don't have anything to announce on that front.
We're watching the timeline closely along with the rest of the business,
but no decisions have been made. If anything changes that affects the
team, we'll communicate it directly rather than letting people hear about
it secondhand."

Q: Will the office snack budget change with the new CFO?
A (Jordan Kim, CFO): Referred to Facilities — no budget changes currently
planned for office snacks.
