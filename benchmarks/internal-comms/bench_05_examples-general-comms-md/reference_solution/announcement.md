**Bottom line:** All DevPortal users must re-link their accounts to Okta SSO
by August 14, 2026, 5:00 PM ET, or they'll lose access until IT manually
restores it.

DevPortal has run its own login system since 2019. We're moving it to the
company's Okta-based SSO to close a finding from the Q3 security compliance
audit.

To re-link your account, go to
https://wiki.example-corp.internal/devportal-sso-migration and click
"Relink Account." It takes about two minutes.

If you miss the deadline, your account will be locked out of DevPortal
until IT restores it manually, which can take 1-2 business days.
