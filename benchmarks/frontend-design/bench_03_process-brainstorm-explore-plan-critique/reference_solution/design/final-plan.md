# Final Design Plan — Aria

## Color
- Bronze Black — #1B1714 — page background
- Brass — #B08D57 — primary accent, rules, icon strokes
- Steam White — #F5EFE3 — headline and body text on dark ground
- Gauge Red — #8C2F2F — pressure-critical accents (the lever's marker)
- Walnut — #4A2E22 — card surfaces and the lever handle graphic

## Type
- Display: Playfair Display — hero headline and section titles
- Body: Source Sans Pro — paragraphs, labels, form copy

## Layout
A centered hero with headline and sub-line, followed by a three-column
feature grid, then a closing waitlist band.

```
+--------------------------------------+
|              header/nav              |
+--------------------------------------+
|            centered hero              |
|          headline + subline           |
+--------------------------------------+
|  feature  |  feature  |   feature    |
+--------------------------------------+
|          waitlist / signup            |
+--------------------------------------+
```

## Signature
Slug: lever-pull
A brass-and-walnut lever graphic sits beside the hero headline. On
hover or keyboard focus it pulls down and springs back, the way the
machine's own lever does, and the gauge marker beside it swings toward
9 bar as it moves.
