Hero: I opened with the shipping-container origin story as the actual headline instead of a slogan, because "hand-bottled, forty at a time, in a shipping container" is the one fact about Ember & Ash that no larger competitor could honestly claim, so it does more work than any generic "bold flavor, small batch" tagline.
Typography: Headings use Anton, a condensed industrial stencil-adjacent display face that reads like warehouse signage or a shipping manifest, while body copy uses Lora, a warmer humanist serif, so the page pairs the container's cold industrial exterior with the warmth of the food actually made inside it.
Structure: Each sauce is labeled only by its heat level (Mild / Medium / Fierce) rather than a position number, because the brief is explicit that customers choose one sauce based on how much heat they want for a dish, not a ranked lineup to work through in order.
