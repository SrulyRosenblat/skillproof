Est. 2019 · Portland, Oregon

Ember & Ash bottles its heat by hand, forty at a time, in a shipping container behind a Portland warehouse.

Founded by two former line cooks who wanted a kitchen no landlord could take away, Ember & Ash still runs every batch through the same converted container - hand-cooked, hand-bottled, hand-labeled.

Three sauces, each built for a different amount of heat you actually want on the plate - pick the one that matches your dish, not a set to work through.

Mild

Verde

Tomatillo, serrano, lime · 1,200 SHU

Bright and grassy with a quick, clean finish - built for eggs and fish tacos.

Medium

Rojo

Fresno chile, roasted garlic, smoked paprika · 8,500 SHU

Slow-building warmth with a smoky sweetness that lingers under the heat.

Fierce

Negro

Ghost pepper, black garlic, charred onion · 94,000 SHU

A brief sweetness gives way to a wall of heat that doesn't let go.
