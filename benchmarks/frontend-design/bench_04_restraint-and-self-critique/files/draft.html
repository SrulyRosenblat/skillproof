What we press

Warble Press has run three lacquer lathes out of a converted mill building in Providence, Rhode Island since 2016. We press up to 500 records a day across two shifts, with a 6-week turnaround from lacquer cut to finished box. Every order starts at a 100 record minimum run, and we mix over 30 custom vinyl color blends in-house so no two labels' pressings look alike.
